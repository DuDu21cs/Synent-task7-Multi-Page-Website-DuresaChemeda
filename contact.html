FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

How long does a typical project take? +
It depends on the scope. A simple website takes 1–2 weeks. A full web app or mobile app typically takes 4–8 weeks. We'll give you a detailed timeline after our initial consultation.
Do you offer payment plans? +
Yes! We typically split projects into 3 payments: 30% upfront, 40% at mid-point, and 30% on delivery. For larger projects, we can arrange custom payment schedules.
What information do you need to get started? +
We'll need a brief description of your project, your target audience, preferred timeline, and budget range. A mood board or reference websites are helpful but not required.
Do you provide support after launch? +
Absolutely. All projects include at least 30 days of post-launch support. We also offer ongoing maintenance plans for long-term support, updates, and monitoring.
Can you work with clients outside Ethiopia? +
Yes! We work with clients globally. We communicate via email, Slack, and video calls, and we adapt to your timezone to ensure smooth collaboration.
