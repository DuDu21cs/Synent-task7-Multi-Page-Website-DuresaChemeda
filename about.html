Our Story

From a Small Team to a Trusted Agency

DuDu Studio was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: build digital products that actually work for people. What started as two developers working from a small office in Bishoftu, Ethiopia, has grown into a full-service digital agency.

Over the years, we've worked with startups, SMEs, and enterprise clients across 15+ industries — helping them launch products, scale platforms, and grow their online presence.

We believe great software is built on strong relationships. Every project starts with listening, understanding your goals, and then crafting a solution that fits perfectly.
